# Title

Gas Town Refinery #1 - Watch Steve Yegge use Jane Austen to run Gas Town!

# Description

In this inaugural episode of the Gas Town Refinery video podcast, Chris Sells
talks to Steve Yegge about the origin of, and future for, the Beads, Gas Town
and Gas City projects. And then, as if that weren't enough, Steve gives an
extended demo of how he uses Gas Town to build Gas Town. Don't miss this video!
