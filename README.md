# Gas Town Hall Community

The unofficial community hub for [Steve Yegge's Gas Town](https://steve-yegge.medium.com/welcome-to-gas-town-4f25ee16dd04) multi-agent orchestrator.

![Welcome to Gas Town](images/welcome-to-gastown.webp)

## What is Gas Town Hall?

Gas Town Hall is a community-run initiative to support developers working with Gas Town. We provide:

- **Documentation & Tutorials** - Helping you get started and level up
- **Real-time Help** - Discord community for troubleshooting and discussion
- **Showcase** - Celebrating what the community builds

## A Word of Warning

Gas Town requires **Stage 6-7+ experience** with AI coding agents. From Steve:

> Gas Town is an industrialized coding factory manned by superintelligent robot chimps, and when they feel like it, they can wreck your shit in an instant.

If you're not there yet, no worries - join the community, learn, and level up.
